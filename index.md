# Privacy Policy for Before You Go Checklist

Effective Date: June 17, 2026

Before You Go Checklist is a simple checklist app that helps users prepare before leaving for travel, work, sports, daily routines, and other activities.

## Information We Collect

Before You Go Checklist does not require an account, login, email address, phone number, or personal profile.

The checklists, checklist items, edits, and progress you create in the app are stored locally on your device. We do not upload, sell, or store your checklist content on our own servers.

## Local Data

The app stores checklist data locally on your device so your lists remain available when you close and reopen the app.

This local data may include:

* Checklist names
* Checklist items
* Checked or unchecked item status
* Customized checklist changes

You can delete your checklists inside the app at any time.

## Sharing

The app allows you to share a checklist using the native iOS share sheet. Sharing only happens when you choose to share. The shared content is sent to the app or service you select, such as messaging, email, or another installed app.

Before You Go Checklist does not control how third-party apps process shared content after you choose to share it.

## Advertising

Before You Go Checklist may display ads using Google AdMob.

Google AdMob may collect or process certain data for advertising, measurement, fraud prevention, and service improvement. This may include device information, advertising identifiers, approximate location, app interaction data, and diagnostic information, depending on your device settings, consent choices, and applicable laws.

For more information about how Google uses data, please review Google’s privacy policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services, including:

* Google AdMob for advertising
* Apple iOS services for sharing and app functionality

These third-party services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

## Children’s Privacy

Before You Go Checklist is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children. The app does not include account registration, public profiles, chat, or user-generated public content.

## Data Security

Checklist data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate a backend database for your checklist content.

Please note that no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

## Your Choices

You can:

* Delete checklist data inside the app
* Delete the app to remove locally stored app data from your device
* Manage advertising privacy settings through your iOS device settings

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
